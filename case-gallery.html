About the gallery

The case gallery accompanies the BAMS CLAMPS facility review. It highlights days that illustrate the breadth of boundary-layer and mesoscale phenomena captured by the mobile profiling system — from nocturnal low-level jets and stable layers to sea breezes, convection initiation, and wind-farm wakes. Cases are selected for scientific representativeness and figure clarity, not as a comprehensive archive of all CLAMPS data.

Each case page presents a four-panel instrument overview (thermodynamics, moisture, winds, and vertical-velocity variance) plus optional auxiliary figures and interpretive notes. A supplemental PDF and figure-reproduction notebook are also available.
