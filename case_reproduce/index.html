Example output

The notebook walks through each step explicitly: locate bundled NetCDF files, read variables, compute θ_v and σ²_w, overlay fuzzy PBLH and cloud-base markers, and build the four matplotlib panels. Inputs are pre-staged — WINDoe and fuzzy PBL are not re-computed in the notebook.
